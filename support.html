How do I get help?

Before the Internet, most customer support happened over the phone or in person. To keep that experience, we offer simple, direct ways to reach us.

  • Phone: 1-800-555-1930 (Mon–Fri, 8am–6pm)
  • Mail: The Daily Chronicle, 123 Print Avenue, Paper City

Common issues

My newspaper did not arrive.

Please call our support line before 10:00am and we will arrange a replacement copy or extend your subscription.

I need to change my delivery address.

Contact support with your current and new address. Changes usually take effect within two business days.

I want to pause my subscription.

We can temporarily stop delivery while you are away and resume it on the date you choose.
