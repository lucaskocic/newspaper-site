Who is behind this newspaper?

The Daily Chronicle Publishing Co. is a family-owned news organization that began as a small local paper in 1952. Long before the Internet, it built its reputation by covering community stories that larger outlets overlooked.

Our mission

Our mission is to provide clear, responsible reporting that helps readers understand both their neighbourhood and the wider world. We aim to balance tradition with thoughtful adaptation to new technologies.

Our values

  • Accuracy and fact-checking
  • Independence from political parties
  • Respectful coverage of diverse communities
  • Commitment to local journalism
